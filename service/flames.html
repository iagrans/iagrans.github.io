FLAMES High School Outreach

Project Description

Created in 2015 as an extension of The Engineering Place summer camp programs, FLAMES originally targeted rising 9th and 10th grade female participants. With over 100 students completing the FLAMES program, the monicker has updated to be inclusive of all genders. FLAMES is open to all high school students in Wake County, and focuses on building student skills in programming using languages like Python and JavaScript.
