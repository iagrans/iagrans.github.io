My main interest is making education more enjoyable and understandable. I am currently helping out with research on Crystal Island, a computer game that teaches 8th graders microbiology. In the future I will probably be working in something close to intellimedia or serious games. I don't want to be a teacher, but I want to help teachers teach, if that makes any sense.

My activities include being an active member of NC State's STARS Alliance (Students & Technology in Academia, Research, and Services) and SPARCs (Students in Programming, Robotics & Computer Science). STARS is geared toward recruiting and retaining women and minorites in compututing, and with SPARCS we go to centennial middle school and teach about different aspects of computing. I am also in NCSU's Scholars Program.

In addition, I am a member of the American Legions Auxillary, and a proud supporter of relay for life and make-a-wish.
